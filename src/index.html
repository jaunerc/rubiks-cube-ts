Rubik's Cube

Rotate the Rubik's cube faces with your keyboard!
Press the keys below for a clockwise rotation or the same key upper-case for a counterclockwise rotation.

On the z-axis:
`f` Front
`m` Middle
`k` Back

On the y-axis:
`t` Top
`e` Middle
`b` Bottom

On the x-axis:
`r` Right
`s` Middle
`l` Left
